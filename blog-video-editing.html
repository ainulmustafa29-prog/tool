Introduction to Video Editing

Video editing is the process of manipulating and rearranging video shots to create a new work. Modern AI tools make video editing more accessible than ever.

Essential Video Editing Concepts

  • Cutting: Removing unwanted sections
  • Trimming: Adjusting clip start and end points
  • Transitions: Effects between clips
  • Color Correction: Adjusting color and exposure

Best Practices

  1. Always work with high-quality source footage
  2. Keep backups of original files
  3. Use appropriate video formats for your platform
  4. Optimize file sizes for web delivery
